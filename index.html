ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was founded in 1950 Columbia,SC by Mr.Robinson and has been a family business since then. We want to give our community the best food and drinks at affordable prices with a friendly atmosphere.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order all day-breakfast menu, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist: Liam Brown

Owner: Angel Hunt

Opening hours: everyday from 7am to 5pm.

Address: 20 bld street, 29202, SC
